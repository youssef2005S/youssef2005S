Who I Am

I'm Youssef Serboute, an engineering student at ENSA Safi, Morocco. My path into engineering started with a Baccalaureate in Physical Chemistry (PC), followed by a preparatory year in MIP at FSSM Marrakech before joining ENSA Safi.

I work across the full stack — from writing Python scripts and C programs to building web interfaces with HTML, CSS and JavaScript, and running simulations in MATLAB. I also do 3D modeling and CAD design using CATIA.

Beyond technical work, I love bringing people together around science — I organized an Astronomy Day event that united curious minds around the wonders of the universe.
